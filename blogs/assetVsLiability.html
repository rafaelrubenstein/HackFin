Asset vs Liability

What is an assest and what is a liabilty? in order to fully undertsand how to invest and how to save money, one must first learn what an asset and liabilty is, and how to identify what is an assest and what is a liabilty.
In the simpilest terms, an asset is anything that has value and makes you money(for example a house you rent out, or a stock).A liability is anything that loses you money (it can have value as well, like a house). So the question is, how do i identify whats an asset and what is a liability? Follow these rules to classify an asset. these rules will not tell you what is a good asset (discussed elsewhere)
  • it has value
  • youre not always only losing money, you can make financial gains on it
  • it puts money in your pocket
To identify an liability follow these rules.
  • it has value
  • you can not make any finincial gains on it
  • it increaces your debt
  • it take money out of your pocket
in conclusion, be very smart with your purchases. budget and plan accordingly, and remember just because it makes you money doesnt mean its a good asset!
